Wedding Planning Timeline

Your step-by-step guide to planning a destination wedding

12-18 Months Before

  • Set your wedding date and budget
  • Book your venue and accommodations
  • Send save-the-date cards
  • Hire wedding planner

6-12 Months Before

  • Book vendors (photographer, decorator, caterer)
  • Plan wedding theme and decor
  • Arrange guest transportation
  • Send formal invitations

3-6 Months Before

  • Finalize menu and entertainment
  • Plan pre-wedding events
  • Book spa and beauty services
  • Confirm guest list and RSVPs

1-3 Months Before

  • Final venue walkthrough
  • Confirm all vendor details
  • Plan seating arrangements
  • Prepare welcome kits for guests

Wedding Week

  • Rehearsal and final checks
  • Welcome guests and distribute kits
  • Enjoy pre-wedding celebrations
  • Relax and celebrate your big day!
