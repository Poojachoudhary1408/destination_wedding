India - A Destination Wedding Paradise

India embodies unique traditions in its diverse cultures. It has breathtaking views of the mighty mountains, pristine beaches, and majestic palaces that add to its glamour. It is becoming a preferred destination wedding choice for couples.

Unique traditions coupled with rich culture

Each state of India takes pride in its age-old traditions backed by the beliefs and sentiments of its ancestors. The festivities, beginning from Haldi, Mehendi, and Sangeet to Baraat, Pheras, and Vidai, symbolise the beauty of the vibrant and splendid Indian culture.

Panoramic Locations

From the tropical beaches of Goa to the royal forts in Rajasthan and the serene backwaters of Kerala, each destination promises an enchanting wedding experience you and your guests will remember forever.

Experiences to Look For

Palatial Grandeur

The regal cities of Udaipur and Jodhpur offer revamped forts and palaces serving as destination wedding resorts. The magnificent architecture and luxurious atmosphere are perfect for those dreaming of an imperial experience.

Beachside Magic

Host a destination wedding on the sandy Anjuna and Calangute beaches of Goa or the pristine beaches of Kerala. Imagine a beautiful sunset with green-blue waters shimmering in your wedding pictures.

Mountain Serenity

Head to the snowy mountains of Kasauli, Manali, Shimla, or Gulmarg to solemnise your wedding across the cold landscapes and literal paradise on earth.

Planning Your Dream Day

A destination wedding is synonymous with an exotic location and aesthetically decorated luxury wedding venues. Finalising a site takes thoughtful planning.

Mindful Budgeting

Setting a budget for a destination wedding in India is a prudent decision. Yet, be flexible to accommodate deviations as you go along with the desire to give lavish experiences to your guests.

Guest Comfort

Preparing a guest list well in advance sets your search direction toward a property that comfortably accommodates everyone. Confirmation of guest lists helps in making seamless travel and accommodation arrangements.
