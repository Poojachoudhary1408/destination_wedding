Wedding Planning

How far in advance should I start planning my destination wedding?

We recommend starting your destination wedding planning at least 12-18 months in advance. This timeline allows you to:

  • Secure your preferred venue and date
  • Book the best vendors (photographers, decorators, caterers)
  • Give guests ample time to make travel arrangements
  • Plan and execute all pre-wedding events
  • Handle legal requirements and documentation

However, we have successfully planned beautiful weddings in as little as 6 months for couples with flexible dates and requirements.

What services does DreamKnot Weddings provide?

We offer comprehensive end-to-end wedding planning services including:

  • Destination selection and venue booking
  • Complete event design and decor
  • Vendor coordination (catering, photography, entertainment)
  • Guest management and travel arrangements
  • Budget planning and management
  • On-site coordination and execution
  • Legal documentation assistance
  • Pre-wedding and post-wedding event planning
Do you handle destination weddings outside India?

Yes! While we specialize in Indian destination weddings, we also plan celebrations in popular international locations including Thailand, Bali, Dubai, Maldives, and Europe. Our global network of trusted vendors ensures the same quality service worldwide.

Budget & Pricing

What is the average cost of a destination wedding?

Destination wedding costs vary significantly based on location, guest count, and services. Here's a general breakdown:

  • Intimate Wedding (50-100 guests): ₹5-10 Lakhs
  • Medium Wedding (100-300 guests): ₹15-30 Lakhs
  • Grand Wedding (300+ guests): ₹30 Lakhs and above

We work with all budgets and can create customized packages to match your requirements. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific needs.

What's included in your wedding packages?

Our packages typically include:

  • Venue booking and management
  • Decor and theme design
  • Catering services
  • Photography and videography
  • Entertainment arrangements
  • Guest accommodation coordination
  • On-site wedding coordination team

We offer flexible packages that can be customized based on your specific requirements and budget.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to make your dream wedding more manageable. Typically, we follow a milestone-based payment structure:

  • 25% advance upon booking
  • 35% three months before the wedding
  • 30% one month before the wedding
  • 10% final payment after the event

Venues & Locations

How do I choose the right destination for my wedding?

Consider these factors when choosing your destination:

  • Budget: Different destinations have varying cost structures
  • Guest accessibility: Choose locations easy for most guests to reach
  • Season & weather: Research the best time to visit
  • Venue options: Ensure adequate choices for your guest count
  • Activities: Consider entertainment options for guests
  • Personal preference: Choose a place meaningful to you

Our team provides personalized consultations to help you select the perfect destination.

Can I visit the venue before booking?

Absolutely! We highly recommend venue site visits and can arrange them for you. We offer:

  • Guided venue tours with our team
  • Virtual tours for international or distant locations
  • Detailed photo and video documentation
  • Meetings with venue managers and vendors
What are the most popular wedding destinations in India?

Our top destination wedding locations include:

  • Goa: Beach weddings and tropical vibes
  • Udaipur: Royal palace weddings
  • Jaipur: Heritage forts and palaces
  • Kerala: Backwater and nature weddings
  • Jim Corbett: Wildlife and adventure
  • Lonavala: Hill station celebrations

Travel & Logistics

Do you help with guest travel and accommodation?

Yes! We provide complete guest management services including:

  • Group flight bookings at discounted rates
  • Hotel room blocks and accommodation arrangements
  • Airport transfers and local transportation
  • Welcome kits and guest information packets
  • 24/7 guest support during the wedding
What legal documents are needed for a destination wedding?

Required documents vary by location and type of ceremony. Generally, you'll need:

  • Valid passports and visas (for international destinations)
  • Birth certificates
  • Proof of address
  • Marriage license application
  • Divorce decree (if applicable)

Our team assists with all legal documentation and ensures compliance with local regulations.

How many days should we plan for a destination wedding?

Most destination weddings span 2-4 days, including:

  • Day 1: Guest arrival and welcome dinner
  • Day 2: Pre-wedding ceremonies (Mehendi, Sangeet)
  • Day 3: Main wedding ceremony and reception
  • Day 4: Post-wedding brunch and departure

We can customize the timeline based on your traditions and preferences.
