About DreamKnot Weddings

Creating unforgettable destination wedding experiences since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, DreamKnot Weddings began with a simple vision: to transform destination weddings into extraordinary celebrations that couples and their families cherish forever.

What started as a small team of passionate wedding planners has grown into India's premier destination wedding company, having orchestrated over 1,000 magical celebrations across stunning locations worldwide.

Our expertise lies in understanding that every couple has a unique story, and we pride ourselves on creating personalized experiences that reflect your love, culture, and dreams.
